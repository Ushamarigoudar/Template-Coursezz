About the concept of copyright-free-images


Copyright-free images are images that are free from any restrictions on use, meaning that they can be used for any purpose without the need for permission or payment.


Using copyright-free images can be a great way to enhance your website or other digital media without having to worry about legal issues or licensing fees.

A copyright-free image is provided by a photographer or owner, and can be used by any person. The photographer or owner owns the copyright but provides the legal authority that the image can be freely used by others. You can usually find copyright-free images on public domains or copyright-free image libraries.
